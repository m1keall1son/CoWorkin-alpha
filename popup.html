Welcome to Co-Workin'!

Co-Workin' is a motivational, team building Chrome Extension that ambiently connects co-workers together. Once you click the coffee mug at the top right tool bar of Chrome you will be co-working and you'll be able to see your fellow coworkers in the pop-out panel located at the lower left corner of any webpage, but first, we need to access some info.
